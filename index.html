Question

Any affordable hotels near Bromo?

Yes, there are several options ranging from guesthouses to mid-range hotels in the nearby village of Cemoro Lawang. Some popular choices include Café Lava Hostel, Yoschi’s Guesthouse, and Lava View Lodge. These places offer basic but comfortable accommodations with stunning views of the Bromo landscape.

Question

Do I need to book a jeep in advance?

While you can arrange a jeep on-site, it’s often recommended to book in advance, especially during peak seasons. Booking ahead ensures you have a vehicle ready at your preferred time and can avoid any potential delays or shortages.

Question

Can I easily rent a motorbike to explore Bromo?

Yes, motorbikes can be rented in Cemoro Lawang and other nearby villages. It’s a popular option for exploring the area independently. Make sure you’re comfortable with the terrain, as the roads can be challenging.

Question

Are there any ATMs close to the Bromo area?

There are a few ATMs in Cemoro Lawang, but it’s recommended to bring enough cash with you, as they can sometimes run out of money, especially during busy periods. Credit card facilities are limited."
